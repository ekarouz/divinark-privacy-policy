Introduction

At Divinark Digital ("we," "our," or "us"), we deeply value your privacy. This Privacy Policy for Divinark remains effective as of January 01, 2025, and outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us. We are dedicated to ensuring your information is handled with transparency and care.

Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information to enhance your experience with our services.

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Billing Details
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide while contacting us or utilizing our services.

Non-Personal Information

  • IP Address

User-Generated Content

Any content you submit through our website, social media, or other platforms will also be collected.

How We Use Your Information

The information we gather serves several purposes:

  • Service Provisioning: We use this information to provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • Communication: Your information allows us to keep you informed of inquiries, updates, and promotional content.
  • Website Improvement: We aim to continuously improve our website, services, and user experience based on your feedback and usage patterns.
  • Compliance: Ensuring our practices align with legal and regulatory requirements is a priority.

Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. However, there are a few circumstances where we may share this information:

Service Providers

We may share your personal data with third-party vendors who support our service delivery, including:

  • Hosting Services
  • Payment Processing
  • Analytics Providers

Legal Authorities

In cases where it is required by law or necessary to protect our rights, we may disclose your information.

Business Transfers

If a merger, sale, or asset transfer occurs, your information may be shared as part of that business transaction.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, it is essential to recognize that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and while we strive for the best security practices, absolute protection cannot be guaranteed.

Your Rights and Choices

As a user, you have rights concerning your personal information. You can:

  • Access: Request to view your personal information we hold.
  • Update: Ask us to update or correct any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Delete: Request the deletion of your personal information where applicable.
  • Opt-Out: Choose to opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Restrict Processing: Restrict or object to specific data processing activities.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at team@divinark.com.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

At Divinark, we utilize cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand how users interact with our website, enabling continuous improvement and personalization.

You can control cookie settings through your browser. However, disabling cookies may impact the functionality of some website features.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We do not control these external sites and are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites before sharing personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
